Paper Cutz

Event Details

Event date: 11 October 2024

Event location: Fairfax, VA

What is it?

Paper Cutz is a battlebot competition with a twist. Build a robot that's good at beating up other robots, except, it's only allowed to be made out of cardboard and masking tape.

Rules

  1. Bots must be constructed exclusively from cardboard and masking tape.
  2. Bots can have up to three electic motors plus control circuitry.
  3. Drivers must be under 12 years old.
